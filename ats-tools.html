Quick ATS Optimization Tips

1

Standard Headings

Always use "Experience", "Education", and "Skills". ATS systems look for these specific headings to categorize information.

2

Keyword Matching

Use exact phrases from the job description. If they ask for "Project Management", don't write "Led Projects".

3

Simple Formatting

Use single column layouts. Avoid tables, columns, and text boxes which can confuse ATS parsing algorithms.

4

File Format

Save as .docx for best ATS compatibility or PDF if it's a text-based PDF. Avoid images and graphics.

5

Contact Information

Place contact info at the top in simple text format. Avoid headers/footers which may not be parsed correctly.

6

Font Choice

Use standard fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman. Avoid decorative fonts that may not parse correctly.
